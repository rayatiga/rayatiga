Fix 'We can't reach the Adobe servers' Error.

Error message show up while trying to open up Adobe XD or other Adobe products in Microsoft Windows 10/11.

Error Information

When I opened Adobe XD, I encountered an error like this:

"We can't reach the Adobe servers. This may be because you're not connected to the internet. Check your connection and try again below. If you're still having issues, please see our connectivity troubleshooting guide."

In fact, my internet is normal. Maybe this just might not happen in Adobe XD. You know what? Surprisingly, after that message, Adobe XD can still be used as usual.

How to Fix

To do this, simply delete the Adobe Web Credential in the Credential Manager.

  1. Go to Search or Control Panel.
  2. Find Credential Manager.
  3. In the Web Credential, You should see like Adobe App Info (â€¦)
  4. Expand on that web credential.
  5. Then, Remove.
After Fixing

After removing the Web Credential from Adobe, this problem will be resolved and no errors appear as before.

And that's it. Problem should be solved!
