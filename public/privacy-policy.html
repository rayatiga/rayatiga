Privacy policy.

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how Rayatiga collects, uses, and protects your personal information while you use our services.

Privacy commitment

We prioritize your privacy and ensure your personal information is collected, used, and protected responsibly.

Data Collection

Rayatiga collects various types of personal and non-personal information to provide, improve, and personalize our services. The information we collect includes:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, billing details, and other information provided when you use our services or contact us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather insights about how you use our site.

Use of Information

The data we collect is used for specific purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain our services, including responding to inquiries and managing service requests.
  • To improve our website and services by analyzing user behavior and preferences.
  • To communicate with you about updates, promotions, or other relevant information.
  • To ensure compliance with legal obligations and protect our business from fraud or abuse.

Data Sharing

Rayatiga does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • With service providers and partners who assist us in operating our business, such as payment processors and hosting providers.
  • If required by law, such as responding to court orders or legal processes.
  • To protect Rayatiga's rights, users, or property in cases of suspected fraud or malicious activity.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data and implement industry-standard measures to safeguard it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. This includes encryption, secure storage, and restricted access protocols. However, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection of your data.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information stored with us.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional communications at any time.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

To exercise these rights, please contact us directly using the details provided below.

Cookies Policy

Rayatiga uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may limit certain features of our site.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Rayatiga reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be communicated through our website, and continued use of our services implies acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@rayatiga.com. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
