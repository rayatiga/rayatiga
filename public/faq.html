Common questions

Get quick answers to the most frequently asked questions about our services, processes, and policies.

What services does Rayatiga offer?

Rayatiga provides domain registration, static website development, support and maintenance, and cloud solutions tailored to meet your business needs.

How do I register a domain with Rayatiga?

Simply contact us with your preferred domain name. We'll guide you through availability checks, registration, and setup for your website.

What is included in website development?

Our static website development includes design, coding, and basic SEO setup. We ensure your site is responsive, fast, and user-friendly.

How does support and maintenance work?

Our support plans cover updates, bug fixes, and performance optimization. Requests outside the scope may be billed separately.

Are your cloud solutions secure?

Yes, we implement robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls.

Can I upgrade or change services later?

Absolutely! Rayatiga allows flexible upgrades or modifications to meet your evolving business needs.

How can I contact support?

You can reach us via email or phone for any inquiries or technical assistance. Response times depend on your selected support plan.

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis, depending on the terms of the service agreement and the progress of the project.

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and select online payment platforms. Invoices are sent electronically for your convenience.

Visit our website or subscribe to our newsletter for updates on services, policies, and offerings.
