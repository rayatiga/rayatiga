Google Ads Instructor Planner

Descriptions

Google Ads Instructor Planner, you will play a crucial role in shaping the skills and knowledge of our team and clients regarding Google Ads.

You will be responsible for designing and delivering high-quality training sessions, developing educational materials, and providing strategic planning for Google Ads campaigns.

Your expertise will help individuals and businesses maximize their online advertising potential.

09:00 AM - 04:00 PM | One time position | Remote work | IDR 9.500.000 - IDR 11.500.000

This position is open until November 24, 2024.

Responsibilities

  • Curriculum Development: Create comprehensive training programs on Google Ads, covering topics such as account setup, campaign management, keyword research, ad copywriting, and analytics.
  • Instructional Delivery: Conduct engaging training sessions, workshops, and webinars for various audiences, ensuring that content is accessible and informative.
  • Performance Analysis: Analyze the effectiveness of Google Ads campaigns and provide actionable insights to improve performance and ROI.
  • Support and Mentorship: Offer ongoing support to trainees, answer questions, and provide guidance on best practices in Google Ads.
  • Stay Updated: Keep up with the latest trends, tools, and updates in Google Ads and online advertising, incorporating this knowledge into training materials.
  • Collaborate: Work closely with the marketing team to align Google Ads strategies with overall business objectives.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Advertising, Business, or a related field.
  • Proven experience in managing Google Ads campaigns with a strong understanding of PPC strategies.
  • Previous teaching or training experience, preferably in a corporate or educational setting.
  • Google Ads certification is required; additional certifications in digital marketing are a plus.
  • Strong presentation and communication skills.
  • Ability to simplify complex concepts for diverse audiences.
  • Proficiency in Google Analytics and other digital marketing tools.
  • Creative thinking and problem-solving abilities.
  • Passionate about teaching and helping others succeed.
  • Detail-oriented with excellent organizational skills.
  • Adaptable and willing to continuously learn and grow.

We Offers

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunities for professional development and training.
  • A collaborative and innovative work environment.
  • Flexible working hours and the possibility of remote work.

Applications

Interested candidates should submit their resume and a cover letter detailing their relevant experience and teaching philosophy to contact@rayatiga.com.
