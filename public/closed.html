Rayatiga Closure Information

We understand this closure may cause inconvenience, and we apologize for any disruption to your service. This page aims to provide further information regarding the closure of Rayatiga.

Closure Date: June 29, 2024

Reason for Closure:

Unfortunately, we cannot disclose the specific reason for Rayatiga's closure at this time. The decision was made after careful consideration.

Reopening:

We currently cannot confirm whether or not Rayatiga will reopen in the future.

What Happens to My Data?

We are committed to protecting your data. As per our terms of service, all user data will be securely deleted following the closure date.

What are my Alternatives?

Depending on the services you utilized on Rayatiga, there may be alternative platforms available. We cannot recommend specific alternatives, but conducting a web search for similar services may be helpful.

How Can I Contact You?

Unfortunately, due to the closure, Rayatiga customer support will no longer be available after June 29, 2024. This website will also become unavailable.

Important Note Regarding Paid Services

If you have purchased any paid services on Rayatiga, please be aware that these services will terminate on their designated expiration date (not end/closed date).

We recommend you download any data or information associated with your paid services (invoices or VAT) before June 29, 2024, as this data will be deleted after the closure.

Access to This Domain and Cloud Services

Please be aware that you will no longer be able to access the Rayatiga domain after May 30, 2024. This includes the website and any associated online account management tools.

Important to note, while access to the Rayatiga domain will be discontinued, certain cloud-based services you may have purchased may still be functional until their designated end date or expiration date, whichever comes first.

Thank You

We appreciate your understanding during this time. We sincerely thank you for choosing Rayatiga and for your support.
