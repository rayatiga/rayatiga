Local Inventory Ads

  • 09:00 - 17.00
  • Remote Work
  • One-time? Position
  • IDR 4.500.000 - IDR 6.500.000

Job Description

The ideal candidate for applying for Google Local Inventory Ads is a forward-thinking business owner or marketing professional with a deep understanding of their local market and a strong online presence.

Key skills: Data Management Analytics SEO Strategic Thinking Local Market Knowledge

Responsibilities

  • Create & maintaining accurate and up-to-date product data feeds, including product attributes, availability, and pricing.
  • Monitoring data feeds for errors and inconsistencies and resolving them promptly.
  • Ensuring data feeds comply with Google Merchant Center data quality requirements.
  • Regularly updating data feeds to reflect changes in product information.
  • Keeping track of in-store inventory levels and ensuring sufficient stock to fulfill orders generated by Local Inventory Ads.

Qualifications

  • A degree in marketing, business administration, or a related field is strongly recommended.
  • Relevant coursework in digital marketing, advertising, and marketing analytics is highly valuable.
  • Experience working with data spreadsheets and databases.
  • Familiarity with data manipulation and analysis techniques.
  • Understanding of data quality and data integrity principles.
  • Experience with Google Merchant Center and other Google Ads platforms is highly beneficial.
