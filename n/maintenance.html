Overview

Free maintenance refers to the complimentary ongoing support provided to clients after the initial website development.

This service is designed to keep your website functioning optimally, ensuring it remains relevant and up-to-date.

As part of our commitment to quality, Rayatiga offers free maintenance with every web development package, allowing clients to make minor updates and enhancements to their websites without incurring additional costs.

The Benefits

With free maintenance, clients can request updates such as content changes, design adjustments, or the integration of new features like analytics tools.

This support helps businesses and individuals focus on growing their brand, while Rayatiga takes care of the technical details.

By offering free maintenance, Rayatiga eliminates the need for clients to worry about additional fees for minor adjustments, providing peace of mind and a seamless experience.
